# Implementation Plan: Boredom Analysis Fixes

Based on the comprehensive playtest report, here's the prioritized implementation plan.

## Phase 1: Quick Wins (Highest Impact, Lowest Effort)

### 1. Toast Notification System ⏱️ 2 hours
- Add floating toast messages for Save/Load/Stats
- Visual feedback for all system actions
- **Impact:** 8/10 - Solves major feedback issue

### 2. Preset Build Templates ⏱️ 3 hours
- Add 4-6 preset buttons in character creation
- "Balanced Warrior", "Mighty Champion", "Cunning Scout", "Charismatic Leader"
- **Impact:** 7/10 - Eliminates 20+ clicks of tedium

### 3. Starter Equipment by Origin ⏱️ 2 hours
- Give each origin appropriate starting gear
- Equipment panel won't be empty
- **Impact:** 7/10 - Makes equipment system immediately relevant

### 4. Consequence Tags on Choices ⏱️ 4 hours
- Show [HIGH RISK], [SAFE], [MODERATE REWARD] tags
- Players understand stakes before choosing
- **Impact:** 8/10 - Adds meaningful decision-making

### 5. Rank Progression Display ⏱️ 2 hours
- Show rank titles (Raw Recruit → Common Soldier → Veteran → Sergeant → Knight)
- Celebrate rank changes
- **Impact:** 6/10 - Clear progression milestones

**Total Phase 1: ~13 hours for massive engagement improvement**

## Phase 2: Medium Effort (Next Sprint)

### 6. Full Stats Panel
- Complete character info display
- All stats, conditions, equipment, career

### 7. Pre-Roll Risk/Reward Choices
- "Play it safe" vs "Take a risk" before resolution
- Adds player agency to dice rolls

### 8. Scene-Based Equipment Acquisition
- Find items after battles
- Purchase from merchants
- Receive from patrons

### 9. Meaningful Failure States
- Actual negative outcomes
- Recoverable but consequential

### 10. Scene Type Variety
- Different scene structures
- Combat, Social, Exploration, Time-Pressure

## Phase 3: Major Enhancements (Long-term)

### 11. Equipment-Dependent Story Branches
- Choices require specific equipment
- Equipment becomes strategically important

### 12. Long-Term Consequence Chains
- Early choices affect later scenes
- Butterfly effect across timeline

### 13. Skill-Based Resolution Minigames
- Replace pure RNG with skill elements
- QTE/pattern matching integration
